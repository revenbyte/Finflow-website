Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At FinFlow MW, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our financial tools.

Key Principle: Your Data Stays Yours

Your financial information never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

1. What Information We Collect

FinFlow MW is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we do and don't collect:

Information You Provide

When you use our calculator or cash flow tracker, the financial data you enter is stored locally on your device. We don't have access to this data.

What We Don't Collect

We don't collect personal identification information, bank details, transaction history, or sensitive business data.

Optional Analytics

If you contact us through our website, we collect only the information you provide in the contact form (name, email, message).

2. Local Storage (Your Device)

FinFlow uses your device's local storage to save your calculations and cash flow data. This means:

  • Data stays on your device - We don't upload it to any server
  • You control your data - Clear browser data to remove it
  • Works offline - No internet needed for your data
  • Device-specific - Data doesn't sync across devices (unless you manually transfer)

Technical note: We use localStorage and IndexedDB (browser technologies) that keep data only on your computer/phone.
