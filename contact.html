Frequently Asked Questions

Is FinFlow MW really free?

Yes! FinFlow MW is completely free for all Malawian businesses. We believe financial tools should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need internet to use it?

No! Our calculator works offline. Cash Flow tracker saves data locally on your device. Internet is only needed for initial download.

Can I use it on my phone?

Absolutely! FinFlow is designed mobile-first. It works perfectly on smartphones, which most Malawian business owners use.

Is my data safe?

Your financial data stays on your device. We don't store it on our servers. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I suggest new features?

Yes! We love hearing from users. Use the contact form above or WhatsApp to suggest features you'd like to see.

Do you support other African countries?

Currently focused on Malawi, but we plan to expand to other African countries in the future with local currency support.
