About FinFlow MW

Financial tools made in Malawi, for Malawi businesses

Our Story

FinFlow MW was born from a simple observation: Malawian small businesses struggle with financial management, not because they lack business skills, but because they lack simple, accessible tools.

While international accounting software exists, it's often:

  • Too expensive for local businesses
  • Too complex for daily use
  • Not designed for Malawi Kwacha (MWK)
  • Requires constant internet (a challenge in many areas)

We decided to build something better. FinFlow MW is built by Malawians, for Malawians. Our tools are simple, work offline, use MWK currency, and are completely free.

Proudly Malawian

Our Mission

To empower every Malawian entrepreneur with simple, free financial tools that help them grow their businesses and improve their lives.

A Malawi where every small business owner has the financial knowledge and tools to succeed, creating wealth and opportunities across our nation.
