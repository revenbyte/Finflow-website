Why We Started FinFlow MW

We saw small businesses struggling with paper records, spreadsheets, and expensive accounting software that wasn't built for Africa.

Business owners were spending hours manually tracking sales and expenses, making pricing decisions based on guesswork, and running out of cash unexpectedly.

We built FinFlow MW to change that. Simple, affordable, and works offline.
